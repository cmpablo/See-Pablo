About the Project: These social posts came from minimal assets and very little budget. In order to accomplish what the editorial calendar called for, I illustrated any additional assets needed and used whatever I could find in the office and my home for props.

I'm most proud of the one with the mantel and stockings. The request was to create an interior of Ernie the Keebler Elf's Hollow Tree that exuded holiday warmth complete with blazing fireplace and mantel. I took it upon myself to make the stockings and stuff each of them with a cookie. I hung those from a space heater that I had in my home that, luckily, looked like an old-timey fireplace and added candles on top for a warm glow. Because Ernie is an illustrated asset, I drew in a garland which I carried through to the following Peace on Earth post.
