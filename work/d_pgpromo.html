P&G Promotions

Website Redesign

Visit: promotionpg.com

Role: Designer

About the Project: P&G is a multi-national consumer goods corporation. I was asked to give their promotions site an update but had to keep the original skeleton and the Publix branding prominent. I also needed to come up with a simple responsive solution for the offers carousel. The resulting site carries P&G's primary blue through the navigation and footer while the offers are designed as two halves that can seamlessly stack on top of each other to fit mobile devices.
