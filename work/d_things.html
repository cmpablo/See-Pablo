Role: Designer, Crafter

About the Projects: Papergoods and decorations for a baby shower and a Save the Date for a wedding. The baby shower was an adventure theme inspried by Wes Anderson. I created all of the signage as well as the air balloon and map garlands. The handmade teepee was dual purpose. It held the baby gifts for the party and then was the gift itself for the baby... although their cats used it first until the baby arrived.

The Save the Date was designed for a special couple who married in Toronto. I illustrated the couple with the Toronto skyline in the background using their wedding color as the main accent color.
