Woofer Dog Sharing Service

Website Design

Visit: Woofer Dog Sharing Service

Role: Design Lead, Front-end Developer

Technologies: Vue.js, Materialize, Sequelize, Cloudinary, Node, Express, Axios

About the Project: The Woofer Dog Sharing Service is a essentially Uber for dogs. This was a concept project dreamed up by four web development students who love their dogs. As the Design Lead, I designed the logo, ensured consistency across the pages, and added custom styled elements. On the development side, my biggest contribution was implementing the revolving Vue carousel and focusing on the front-end, but the project was largely a group effort. Save for a couple of navigation quirks, we accomplished more than what we set out to do.
